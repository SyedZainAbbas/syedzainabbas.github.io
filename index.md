## About Me

Welcome! I'm **Syed Zain Abbas**, a Data Scientist and Engineer passionate about applying machine learning and advanced analytics to tackle complex challenges in energy and power systems. I bring broad experience in data engineering, ETL pipelines, database design, optimization, deep learning, and KPI dashboard development. My work bridges research innovation with practical applications, including recent efforts on graph-based generative models for synthetic distribution grid data generation. I thrive on solving challenging problems at the intersection of data science and energy engineering.

### What I Do

- **Data Science & Analytics**: Developing predictive models, performing statistical analysis, and extracting insights from large datasets
- **Machine Learning**: Building and deploying ML models for various applications including classification, regression, and deep learning
- **Engineering**: Creating robust data pipelines, APIs, and scalable software solutions
- **Research**: Contributing to the field through publications and innovative approaches to data problems

### Key Skills

- **Programming**: Python, SQL, JavaScript
- **Data Science & AI**: Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Statistical Analysis, Graph Neural Networks, Time Series Forecasting
- **Libraries & Frameworks**: PyTorch, PyTorch Geometric, TensorFlow, scikit-learn, SciPy, Pandas, NumPy, Polars, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly, Dash, Streamlit, FastAPI, SQLAlchemy
- **Data Engineering**: ETL pipelines, Database Design, Spark, Data Quality Validation (Great Expectations)
- **Databases**: PostgreSQL, MySQL, NoSQL
- **Cloud and DevOps**: AWS Cloud, Docker, Kubernetes, Terraform, Linux
- **Version Control & Collaboration**: Git, GitHub, GitLab, Jira, Confluence, Microsoft Office
